PAU Dress Code Guidelines for Female Students

General Policy

Students are expected to be modestly dressed always. The guiding principles for a good dress sense should be due care for one's professional image and respect for others who may be negatively affected by immodest dressing.

Acceptable Guidelines
  • Knee-length skirts and dresses
  • All shirts and tops must have sleeves
  • Modest necklines (no plunging necklines)
  • Loose-fitting clothing
  • Non-sheer, non-flimsy fabrics
  • Professional footwear (no slippers during regular hours)
  • Smart casual for lectures
  • Business/business casual for official events
Not Acceptable
  • Plunging necklines
  • Skirts/dresses above knee length
  • Sleeveless tops or shirts
  • Tight-fitting clothes
  • Torn jeans
  • Slippers (except after 6pm Mon-Sat, all day Sunday)
  • Shorts, joggers, sweatpants (except after 6pm Mon-Sat, all day Sunday)
  • Sheer or transparent fabrics
  • Sagging clothing
Specific Measurements & Details
Skirt and Dress Guidelines:
  • Hemline: Must be knee-length
  • Front slit: Should not be above the knee
  • Back slit: Should not go more than 2 inches above the knee
  • Note: Pantyhose or tights do not suffice as appropriate cover when the skirt or dress is above the knee
Clothing Fit Guidelines:
  • Loose skirts/trousers: Must be worn with shirts that cover the waist area at all times
  • Fitted skirts: Must be worn with shirts that cover the hip area at all times
  • Skinny/slim trousers: Must be worn with loose shirts covering the thigh area
  • Leggings: Must be worn with loose, knee-length tops
Time-Based Exceptions
Relaxed Hours

Monday to Saturday after 6pm
All day Sundays

During these times, the following are allowed in the academic complex:

  • All footwear (excluding bathroom slippers)
  • Loose Bermuda shorts
  • Sweatpants and joggers (loose fitting)
Sports & Recreation
Sports Attire

For sports and recreational activities within specified areas:

  • Sportswear should be loose and non-clinging
  • May only be used in sports grounds or sports complex
  • Must still maintain modesty standards
Pro Tips for Compliance:
  • When in doubt, choose more conservative options
  • Check your outfit in good lighting before leaving
  • Keep a backup compliant outfit in your hostel
  • Use this AI system to verify compliance
  • Consider the professional context of the university
  • Respect for others' comfort is key
  • Take photos from different angles to check fit
  • Ask friends for a second opinion if unsure
Consequences:
  • Students dressed inappropriately will not be allowed to enter the University
  • Consistent violations will be subject to disciplinary action
  • Missing classes due to dress code violations can affect academic performance
  • Attendance issues may prevent students from sitting for exams
